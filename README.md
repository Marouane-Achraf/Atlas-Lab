# Company Workforce Analysis Dashboard

This repository provides an overview of a **Company Workforce Analysis Dashboard**, including pages for Overview, Demographics, and Attrition. The dashboard is designed to help management understand workforce composition, trends, and turnover patterns.

---

## Overview

The **Overview** page provides a comprehensive snapshot of the companyâ€™s status, serving as an introductory page that presents the big picture without detailed specifics.

### Key Metrics (Left Side)
- Total number of employees
- Number of currently active employees
- Number of inactive employees
- Employee attrition rate

### Employee Trends (Below Key Metrics)
A timeline chart shows **changes in employee count over the years**:
- **Green:** Current number of employees
- **Red bars:** Employees who have left
- **Black numbers:** Total number of employees each year (active + departed)

### Department Distribution (Right Side)
- Chart showing **employee distribution across departments**
- Majority in technical departments, followed by sales and HR

### Role Distribution (Bottom Right)
- Chart showing **employee distribution by job roles within each department**
- Provides insight into the size and role of each position

**Summary:**  
This page provides an overall view of the company, highlighting its size, workforce distribution, and trends over time.

## Demographics

The **Demographics** page focuses on the personal and demographic characteristics of employees.

### Employee Ages (Left Side)
- Oldest employee in the company
- Youngest employee in the company

### Ethnicity & Salaries
- Chart showing **employee count and average salaries across ethnic backgrounds**

### Age Groups (Top Right)
- Chart showing **employee distribution by age groups**

### Marital Status (Below Age Groups)
- Donut chart showing **marital status** (married, single, divorced)

### Gender Distribution (Bottom)
- Chart showing **employee distribution by gender**

**Summary:**  
This page explains workforce composition in terms of age, gender, marital status, and background, helping understand the employee base.

---

## Attrition

The **Attrition** page analyzes employee turnover and its potential causes.

### Key Metrics (Left Side)
- Overall employee attrition rate
- Chart showing **attrition rate by hiring date** to identify high-risk periods

### Department & Role Attrition
- Chart showing **attrition rates by department and job title**

### Travel & Attrition (Top Right)
- Green bars: Employees required to travel vs. not
- Blue line: **Attrition rate compared to business travel rate**

### Tenure Analysis (Bottom)
- Chart showing **attrition based on years of service (tenure)**

**Summary:**  
This page identifies why employees leave, linking turnover to department, job nature, travel requirements, and experience, helping management reduce attrition.
